## Description (up to 4000 characters)

**MyFinPal - Your Personal Finance Companion**

Take control of your financial future with MyFinPal, the simple and private app designed to help you track your net worth and measure your progress toward financial independence.

**Key Features:**

✓ **Net Worth Tracking**
Get a clear picture of your financial health by tracking all your assets and liabilities in one place. See your total net worth at a glance and watch it grow over time.

✓ **Guided Onboarding**
Our TurboTax-style onboarding walks you through discovering all your assets and liabilities step by step. Takes just 5 minutes to get started.

✓ **Category Breakdown**
Organize your finances by category - cash, investments, real estate, vehicles, retirement accounts, mortgages, loans, and more. Visualize your financial picture with clear category breakdowns.

✓ **Private & Secure**
Your financial data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

✓ **Resume Anytime**
Start your financial journey and complete it at your own pace. Your progress is saved, so you can pick up where you left off.

**Coming Soon:**
- Monthly budget tracking
- Spending categories and analysis
- Savings rate calculation
- FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) number calculator
- Progress tracking toward your FI goals
- Coast FI and scenario planning

**Perfect For:**
- Individuals tracking personal net worth
- Couples managing shared finances
- Families planning for financial independence
- Anyone on the FIRE journey

**Why MyFinPal?**
We believe financial tracking should be simple, private, and focused on your goals. MyFinPal is designed to be your trusted companion on your journey to financial freedom - no complicated features, no data selling, just clear insights into your financial health.

Start tracking your net worth today and take the first step toward financial independence.
